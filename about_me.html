I am interested most aspects of intelligent robotic systems (computer vision, motion planning, navigation, cognition), but my main area concerns legged locomotion and the interaction between elasticity-induced passive dynamics and active control.

I approach open questions in this field from a perspective of mechanics, nonlinear control and machine learning.

Besides my research, I enjoy making music, running, and video game development.
